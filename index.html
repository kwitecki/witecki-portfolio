Roommate Animal Personality Type Quiz

Together my Coworker and I created a interactive personality-based roommate personality quiz created for students living in on-campus housing at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF)Similarly to a Buzzfeed-style quiz, it assigns participants to one of several archetypes (Falcon, Koi Fish, Cat, Blue Heeler, or Duck) based on their roommate lifestyle preferences, communication style, cleanliness, study habits, and social behavior. The purpose is to help students better understand their own living habits and provide insight into compatible roommate matches . This quiz is meant to be engaging, entertaining, and informative, ultimately aiding CSUF Housing in community building and roommate pairing, it just for fun so they can few who is compatible with their archetype, it has no overall effect on their housing application
